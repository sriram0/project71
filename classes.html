Classes

On the junior side of GHPS they have Preps (foundation) and Grade 1s.
On the senior side, they have Grade 2s to 6 (They start using chromebooks, a type of computer in Grade 2).
In each year level, they have 2-3 seprate classes with the name of the classes being the year level followed by the first letter of the last name of the teacher. For example, if there was a teacher named Mr. Baxter who was teaching Grades 4s his class would be called 4B. In 2021, due to a lack of teachers, the Grade 5s and 6s have merged into one year level (5-6J, 5-6OP, etc.)
