Events

Swimming Carnival

The Swimming Carnival is for Grades 3 - 6 where they compete against their age group & their gender (students have the option to compete because not everyone can swim in deep water) in different events such as 50m feestyle, 50m backstroke, 100m freestyle, etc. The top 4 in each event within their group will get house points and which ever house gains the most points at the end wins the swimming carnival but before that each house says it's chant and the teachers judge which house sang the loudest and that house gets the spirit cup.Learn more about house points

Cross Country

In the Cross Country event Grades 3 compete against their age group & their gender (all students have to compete) and run 2 laps around Lord Reserve, a park next to the school's senior side, and the students have to run 2-3 laps based on age group (1 lap = 1km). The top 4 in each group will get house points and which ever house gains the most points at the end wins crosss country but before that each house says it's chant and the teachers judge which house sang the loudest and that house gets the spirit cup.Learn more about house points

House Athletics

In the Cross Country event Grades 3 compete against their age group & their gender (all students who bring back the form that will be sent out before will compete) in various events inculing vortex & shotput throw, long & triple jump, and the 100m and 500m races. The top 4 (top 3 will get a badge) in each group will get house points and which ever house gains the most points at the end wins crosss country but before that each house says it's chant and the teachers judge which house sang the loudest and that house gets the spirit cup.Learn more about house points
