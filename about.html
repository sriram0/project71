About GHPS

Glen Huntly Primary School - GHPS is a beautiful government school with lots of events & new things to learn through-out the school year. Each year they review previously-learnt topics to add on to the student's understanding and dig deeper into the topic. Once week each class has Specialists (includes art, library, music/languistics, and phisical education. See subjects for more).
